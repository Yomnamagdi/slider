Chocolate Chip Cookie Cake

This giant cookie cake combines everything you love about chocolate chip cookies with the moist and tender crumb of a buttery pound cake. It's simple to bake up in a bundt pan and will delight cookie-loving kids

Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cake covered in fluffy cream cheese frosting is as visually striking as it is delicious. Feel free to scatter some chopped pecans on top for extra crunch in every slice.

World's Best Carrot Cake

Perfectly moist and delicious carrot cake studded with pineapple and pecans and covered in a rich cream cheese frosting is a hands-down winner when it comes to birthday time. Use small, slender carrots (such as those found at farmer's markets) for the sweetest crumb.

Classic Chocolate Cake

It's hard to find anyone who doesn't love chocolate birthday cake. If you've got a couple of mixing bowls, a whisk, measuring cups, and two cake pans, you can make this rich and decadent chocolate cake that makes good use of common pantry ingredients.

Chocolate Banana Cake

Your guests will go bananas for this rich and moist chocolate layer cake. Use bananas verging on over-ripe for an intensely tropical flavor in the finished cake. You can also add a few chocolate curls on top for a party-worthy presentation.

Fluffy Vanilla Cake

For those who prefer vanilla to chocolate, this light and moist vanilla cake is sure to please. It's easy to make from scratch and can be finished with your choice of frosting and decorated to suit any occasion.
